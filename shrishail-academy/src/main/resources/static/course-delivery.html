Course Delivery Policy

How we deliver our courses and ensure quality learning experiences.

Last updated: February 24, 2026

1. Delivery Modes

Online Classes

  • Conducted via Google Meet
  • Meeting link shared before each session via WhatsApp or email
  • Students need a device with internet access and browser
  • Sessions are interactive with real-time Q&A

Offline Classes

  • Conducted at our center: #662, 1st Floor, 67th Cross, Near Blossom School, Kumaraswamy Layout, Banashankari 1st Stage, Bengaluru – 560078
  • Small batch sizes for personalized attention
  • All necessary study materials provided

2. Class Schedule

  • Schedules are mutually agreed upon during enrollment
  • Classes are held on weekdays and weekends as per student preference
  • Holiday schedule aligned with school calendar
  • Schedule changes require 48 hours advance notice

3. Study Materials

BrightNest Academy provides comprehensive study materials including worksheets, practice papers, reference notes, and digital resources. All materials are proprietary and for enrolled students only.

4. Progress Tracking

  • Regular assessments and tests
  • Monthly progress reports shared with parents
  • Parent-teacher meetings scheduled as needed
  • Dashboard access for enrollment and progress tracking

5. Technical Requirements (Online)

  • Stable internet connection (minimum 5 Mbps)
  • Computer, tablet, or smartphone with camera and microphone
  • Google Chrome browser (recommended)
  • Quiet learning environment
